Familiar for Python users and easy to get started

Dask uses existing Python APIs and data structures to make it easy to switch between Numpy, Pandas, Scikit-learn and their Dask equivalents. You don't have to rewrite your code or retrain to scale up.

Dask respects and engages ecosystem and community standards

Scales up to clusters or just use it on your laptop

Dask scales to thousand-node clusters and its algorithms have been tested on some of the largest supercomputers in the world.

You don't need a cluster though. Many people use Dask to scale data on their laptop, using multiple cores for computation and their disk for excess storage.

Flexible and Extensible letting you parallelize your internal systems

Dask exposes lower-level APIs letting you build custom systems for custom needs. This helps open source community leaders parallelize their own packages and helps businesses parallelize tricky business logic
